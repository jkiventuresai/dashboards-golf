Adopt a Golf Hole

This form is designed to measure local interest in funding golf course improvements. The idea is similar to an “Adopt a Highway” program: golfers, residents, businesses, and community groups could contribute toward restoring individual holes or other high-priority areas at local courses.

Goal: Identify the courses and holes golfers care about most, estimate realistic community funding interest, and prioritize renovation projects that could return neglected golf facilities to top-tier playing condition. Completing this form does not create a binding donation commitment.
